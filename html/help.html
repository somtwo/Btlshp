BTLSHP is a turn-based multiplayer game which pits you against an opponent in a battle of wits and strategy!
This is the grid:
Notice you can't see any enemy ships? Well, they have to be in your radar to see them. Each ship is equipped with radar which lets you see where enemy ships are hiding! Grid squares your ships can see with radar are highlighted on the grid.
Notice that your opponent's ship is highlighted in red and only the parts that are within your radar range are visible.
These guys are reef. You can't go through them. These guys are mines. They will damage your ships! You can only see them when in range of sonar.
